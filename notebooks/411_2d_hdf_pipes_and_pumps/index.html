    Pipe Network Timeseries Data Description¶

    The get_pipe_network_timeseries function returns an xarray DataArray for each variable. Here's a general description of the data structure:

    1. Pipes/Pipe Flow DS and Pipes/Pipe Flow US:
    2. Dimensions: time, location (pipe IDs)
    3. Units: ft^3/s (cubic feet per second)

    4. Description: Represents the flow rate at the downstream (DS) and upstream (US) ends of pipes over time.

    5. Pipes/Vel DS and Pipes/Vel US:

    6. Dimensions: time, location (pipe IDs)
    7. Units: ft/s (feet per second)

    8. Description: Shows the velocity at the downstream (DS) and upstream (US) ends of pipes over time.

    9. Nodes/Depth:

    10. Dimensions: time, location (node IDs)
    11. Units: ft (feet)

    12. Description: Indicates the depth of water at each node over time.

    13. Nodes/Drop Inlet Flow:

    14. Dimensions: time, location (node IDs)
    15. Units: cfs (cubic feet per second)

    16. Description: Represents the flow rate through drop inlets at each node over time.

    17. Nodes/Water Surface:

    18. Dimensions: time, location (node IDs)
    19. Units: ft (feet)
    20. Description: Shows the water surface elevation at each node over time.

    General notes: - The 'time' dimension represents the simulation timesteps. - The 'location' dimension represents either pipe IDs or node IDs, depending on the variable. - The number of timesteps and locations may vary depending on the specific dataset and simulation setup. - Negative values in flow variables may indicate reverse flow direction.
